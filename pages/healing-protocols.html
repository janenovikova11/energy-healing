Core Tools for All Protocols

The White Light Field

Your primary protection and healing medium. White light represents your highest soul frequency — it dissolves what doesn't belong and restores what does.

How to build it: Imagine a bright white sun in the center of your heart. Feel it radiating outward — through your chest, your limbs, your head. Expand it until it fills your whole body. Then expand further, to fill the room, the house. The bigger and brighter you make it, the more effective your session will be.

The 13 Transformations

How to use: Simply imagine the 13 signs (see below) appearing one by one in the white light around your body. You don't need to draw them — intention and imagining is enough. They work on the level of energetic principle, not physical form.

Full Trauma Healing Protocol

Use this when working with a specific trauma, pattern, or painful memory. Set aside 20–40 minutes of uninterrupted time.

1
Prepare your space. Find a quiet place where you won't be disturbed. Sit or lie comfortably. Take a few slow breaths to settle.
2
Build white light protection. White sun in your heart → expands through your body → fills the house. Hold this field throughout the session.
3
Apply the 13 Transformations. Imagine the 13 signs in the white light around your body — preparing the field for deep work.
4
Focus on the trauma. Bring the event or pattern to mind. Don't force — let what surfaces, surface. Notice where you feel it in your body: tightness, heaviness, heat, numbness.
5
Acknowledge it. Say internally or out loud: "I acknowledge this pain. I acknowledge this experience. I acknowledge what I've been carrying." This activates the frozen energy without overwhelming it.
6
Apply 13 Transformations directly to the area of the body where you feel the trauma. Imagine the signs specifically there — restructuring, clearing, restoring.
7
Retrieve any missing energy parts related to this event. Use the Return Your Energy Body Parts protocol now if you sense fragmentation from this specific trauma.
8
Cancel any contracts formed during or because of this event. Say: "I release any contracts, oaths, or agreements formed in connection with this experience. They are complete. I am free."
9
Think of what could have been different. What would you have done differently? What do you wish had happened? This changes the energetic program associated with the trauma.
10
Ground and integrate. Imagine roots growing from your body deep into the earth. Feel the warm, stable energy of the earth rising through your roots. Say: "Power on!" Breathe slowly. Let the integration happen.

Healing Ancestral & Past Life Trauma

When a current-life trauma has roots that run deeper — in your family line or past incarnations — this extended protocol helps address those layers.

1
Begin with your light field and 13 Transformations as always.
2
Ask the question. Once calm and centered, ask internally: "Is this pattern also present in my family line or a past life?" Notice what comes — images, feelings, a knowing.
3
Go to where the trauma started. Allow yourself to sense (not force) the origin. It might be abstract — a color, a feeling tone, a word. Trust whatever comes.
4
Acknowledge and address it there. Apply the 13 Transformations to that origin point. Say: "I heal this pattern at its root. In all timelines, in all dimensions."
5
Release ancestral contracts. Say: "I release any agreements made by my ancestors that no longer serve the highest good of my family line. I heal what they couldn't heal. I carry this no longer."
6
Return to the present. Feel the energy shift from the past back into now. Say: "I am here. I am whole. I am free from what came before."
7
Ground deeply. Roots into the earth. Receive earth's energy. Say: "Power on!" Let the earth anchor you in the present moment.

The Power of Intention in Healing

Healing of any trauma is basically performed through intention. You "just get up" one day and set an intention to heal — something like: "I decide to heal from this."

The key is not just thinking it — it's feeling it in your body as a decision already made.

  • ◆ Thought is magnetic — it attracts
  • ◆ Feeling is magnetic — it draws to you
  • ◆ Spoken word + embodied feeling = maximum healing power

If you feel low energy (tired, sad, demotivated) — there is not enough power to back up your intention. First use the Release Numbness exercise to reconnect with sensation. Then bring the intention.
