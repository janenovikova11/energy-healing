Lacking Energy Body Parts

When trauma is too overwhelming to process, fragments of your energy body can detach and become "frozen" — leaving gaps that drain your vitality and recreate painful patterns.

You will use the 13 Transformations in this protocol — imagine each symbol in sequence in the white light around your body. Full reference with explanations →

What Happens During Trauma

At the moment of overwhelming experience — death of a loved one, violation, extreme fear — your energy body can't hold the full charge. As a protective mechanism, part of it fragments and detaches, staying frozen at that moment in time.

This is called "soul loss" in shamanic traditions. The fragment doesn't disappear — it remains in the energetic space of the event, or gets absorbed into the energy field of the person involved in the trauma.

The abuser also carries your energy unintentionally. And you may be carrying pieces of theirs. This creates an ongoing invisible connection that keeps pulling you back toward similar experiences.

Signs You Have Missing Parts

  • ◆ Feeling chronically "not quite here" or emotionally numb
  • ◆ Low energy that doesn't improve with rest
  • ◆ A specific area of life feels blocked despite your efforts
  • ◆ Recurring physical issues in a particular body area
  • ◆ Feeling like "part of you died" after a significant loss
  • ◆ The person who hurt you still has a strong energetic pull
Important: The person who has your energy parts will also suffer — they carry energy that doesn't belong to them, generating excess charge and exhaustion. Returning parts benefits everyone involved.

Protocol: Return Your Energy Body Parts

Do this in a quiet space, sitting comfortably. Begin with the daily energy protection meditation first if you're new to this work.

1
Create your light field. Imagine a big, bright white light — like a sun — in the center of your heart. Expand it until it fills your entire body, then extend it outward to the size of a house.
2
Identify the person. Imagine the person you suspect has your energy parts — the one connected to the trauma. See them standing in the white light, in front of you.
3
Make the request. Say out loud with clear intention: "Show me if you have any parts of my energy body." Notice what you sense — tingling, a pull, images, or just a knowing.
4
Claim your parts. Say: "I am a human being. I order all contracts, implants, and any forms of imprints to be released from my energy body. I only need parts that belong to me. I demand that my energy body parts return to me. I only need what is mine."
5
Apply the 13 Transformations. Imagine each of the 13 signs (see the Healing Protocols page) in the white light around your body. Your energy brightens as the returning parts integrate.
6
Handle an entity if needed. Sometimes an entity clings to the returning parts. Say: "I am a human being and order to the entity parasitising on my energy that belongs to me to go back to where it came from. Do the 13 Transformations." Then return to step 4.
7
Ground and close. Keep the white light around you. Feel the energy return and settle. Breathe slowly and feel your energy body brighten and become more whole.
If the entity won't let go: Say "I am a human being and I order to the entity parasitising on the energy that belongs to me, go back to where it came from." Imagine the 13 Transformations signs in white light around your body — the entity dissolves, and your parts return to you.
