Daily Practice

Short daily rituals that maintain your energy field, protect you from accumulating foreign energies, and keep your healing momentum going.

The principle: Think of your energy body like your physical body — it benefits from regular maintenance. A 5–10 minute daily practice prevents buildup and keeps your baseline vibration high, making deeper healing sessions more effective.

You will use the 13 Transformations in this protocol — imagine each symbol in sequence in the white light around your body. Full reference with explanations →

Basic Preparation Before Any Healing

These steps form the foundation of every session. With practice, they take less than 2 minutes and become second nature.

1. Find a Safe Space

Choose a quiet place where you won't be disturbed. Sitting or lying — whatever feels most natural. You don't need special equipment, music, or props. Intention and attention are the tools.

2. Calm Your Mind

Take 3–5 slow breaths. Let the mental noise settle. You don't need to reach a perfect meditative state — just a slight pause from autopilot. This creates the inner space for the work.

3. Hold the White Light

Imagine a bright white sun in your heart. Breathe it larger. Fill your body completely. Then expand outward — to the size of the room, the house. Hold this field throughout your practice.

4. Set Your Intention

Before you start, state clearly (out loud or internally) what you're working on today. The more specific, the better. Your subconscious and your energy body respond to clear, directed intention.

Daily Healing Meditation

A complete daily reset — takes 5–10 minutes. Best done in the morning or evening, but any time works.

1
Open your light field. Take time to really feel this: a huge sun of white light right at the center of your chest. Expand it fully through every cell, organ, and layer of your body.
2
Expand the light until it fills the size of the house, huge bright light around you. Feel the warmth and brightness. Spend a full 30 seconds here — the bigger and more vivid, the more effective.
3
Scan your body. Move your attention from feet to crown, slowly. Notice any areas that feel darker, heavier, contracted, or numb. Don't judge — just notice.
4
Focus on your belly area and say out loud, with a very strong intention: "I deactivate and release the primary space of the realisation into my body. A huge flow of white energy comes into my body."
5
Apply the 13 Transformations in the white light around your body. Imagine the 13 signs appearing — each one bringing a different kind of restructuring and repair to your field.
6
Remember good things. Bring to mind 3–5 things that happened today or recently that you feel genuinely grateful for. Let the feeling of gratitude fill your chest. This elevates your frequency for the rest of the day.
7
Ground. Imagine roots growing from your body deep into the earth — like you are a tree. Feel the earth's warm, solid energy rising up through those roots. Say out loud: "Power on!"
8
Reconnect with your life. Take a breath. Move your fingers and toes. Return to the present moment feeling refreshed, whole, and energised. The practice is complete.

Self-Protection in Daily Life

Use this whenever you feel energetically attacked, overwhelmed, or after any intense interaction. It can be done in seconds once you practice it.

1
Flash your light field. In any situation — mid-conversation, on a train, before a difficult meeting — quickly imagine a huge white sun expanding from your heart to the size of the house around you.
2
State your sovereignty. Say internally: "I am a human being and the only energy that may enter me is that which is allowed by my original soul blueprint." This creates a selective energetic boundary.
3
Apply the 13 Transformations in the white light around you — even just imagining them briefly seals your field against foreign intrusion.
4
Check in with your body. Does the heaviness or charge feel lighter? Notice the shift. If you feel energetically attacked, you can also physically shower with the intention of washing all foreign energy away.

Tip: How to Feel Your Intention Working

Remember how it feels when you finally make an important decision — "Do I accept this job offer or not? Should I get married?" When you finally decide, you feel a kind of relief and completeness. A weight lifts.

This is the feeling you're looking for when you give an energetic command. Not forcing — deciding. The moment you feel that click of resolution, the order is complete.

If you can't feel anything — try the Release Numbness exercise first to reconnect your emotional body.

During the Day

Evening (10 min)

Clear all templates from the day. Energy bond maintenance. Gratitude practice. Ground before sleep.
